GameAnalytics Website

First, we focused on where we needed to improve, asking ourselves, “what’s not being said on our website?” And while our old homepage did a decent job explaining what GameAnalytics is, it didn’t always help users understand “the goal” — i.e., how to use the GameAnalytics platform to overcome the problems they face in their daily work. Plus, the last redesign of our homepage took place in 2016, and since then, the product had developed—a lot. GameAnalytics’ website began to feel cobbled together and, ultimately, incomplete.

Main objectives:

  • - Guide our users through the main features and products that will help them to improve their games.
  • - Create a foundation for our product story over the coming years
  • - Create a modern and game-friendly website
  • - Improve GameAnalytics branding by making every aspect of the site consistent with the brand’s personality, from images and layout to the web copy and blog posts
  • Website design
  • Branding
  • Wireframing/Prototyping
